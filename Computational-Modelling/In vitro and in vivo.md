**In vitro and in vivo confirmation of the predictions:**

*in-vitro pro-atherogenenic exposure signatures*
In-vitro derived expression signatures after LPS exposure on bone marrow derived macrophages were generated as described by Salloum et al. (2024) and retrieved from the Gene Expression Omnibus (GSE196188)49. Gene expression signatures following exposure of human plaque-derived myofibroblast to OxLDL were obtained from previously published data as described by Buono et al. (2023)20. In-vitro derived expression signatures after oxPAPC exposure on vascular cell lines were generated as described by Moreau et al. (2021) and retrieved from the Gene Expression Omnibus (GSE136813)50. In-vitro derived expression signatures after IL-1ß exposure on human aortic endothelial cells (HAEC) were generated as described by Raju et al. (2024) and retrieved from the Gene Expression Omnibus (GSE89970)51.

*In-vitro Colchicine Exposure*
Human Coronary Artery Endothelial Cells (HCAEC, Promocell, CAT#C-12221) were cultured until 80 % confluency was reached. Next, HCAEC were seeded in a concentration of 30,000 cells/well in a 24 wells plate and incubated for 24 h at 37 °C, 5 %CO2. The following day HUVECs were treated with 1uM (~400ng/ml) Colchicine in endothelial basal medium MV (EGM-2, Lonza, Catalog #: CC-3162) supplemented with 0.5 % FBS (Corning BV, CAT#2–079-CV) for 24h (n = 2, technical replicates). After, EGM-2 was removed from the cells and fresh EGM-2 with the same stimuli was added. At each time point morphology of the cells was checked and cells were lysed in 300 μl TRIzol Reagent (ThermoFisher, CAT#15596026) for further RNA isolation.
